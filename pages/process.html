Our Process for Making the AI

At EcoFriend, we follow a rigorous process to develop our AI solutions:

  1. Identify the environmental problem we want to solve.
  2. Research and gather data relevant to the problem.
  3. Develop and train machine learning models using the data.
  4. Test and validate the models to ensure accuracy and reliability.
  5. Deploy the AI solutions and monitor their performance.
  6. Continuously improve the models based on feedback and new data.
